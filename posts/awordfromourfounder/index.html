A Word from our Founder

By Dillon de Silva
@dillondesilvaa
July 20, 2020 | 2 minute read
Tags: welcome stem students


Hey, everyone!

Developing Bits to Bytes has been a jam-packed journey for me over the past couple of months. The team and I have iterated over the idea several times, putting in hours of effort into bringing it to life.

Now as I type up this first post, I find myself teeming with excitement at the possibilities of what Bits to Bytes can grow into.

A strong passion for science, technology, engineering and maths has inspired me to come up with this idea. I love to engage with these subjects and share what I learn with the people in my life.

Bits to Bytes is a project for students to share their experiences and insights with STEM. My goal is for this Bits to Bytes to become a hub for all students to access ideas about STEM developed by young people.

A place where anyone can come to for knowledge about STEM or to obtain some skills through interesting projects.

Through our student written posts, YouTube tutorials covering abstract but cool projects and our social streams, we are hoping that we can spark interest into the generation developing the future.

If you’ve got something you think is worth sharing. Perhaps a personal experience or a lesson you’ve learnt along the way.

We want to hear it.

Because here at Bits to Bytes, we value students and STEM.

I believe each and every one of us has the power to learn something about technology and apply it to our own lives.

Join us on the ride!

    - Dillon :)
