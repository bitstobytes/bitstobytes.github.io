Say YES!

By Jemma Swaak
@nameisjemma
September 8 2020 | 2 minute read
Tags: opportunities stem inspiration


Have you ever wondered what it would be like if you just started saying yes to life and all the opportunities that come your way? I know after watching the Yes Man movie (I recommend it!) when I was little I thought the prospect of embracing every experience sounded fun and exciting… yet I really did not realise where it would lead me!

Somehow I became a person who seemingly did so many things and ended up loving meeting people and embracing new adventures. I didn’t start out like this. I wouldn’t have dared imagine that Year 12 me would be so excited to try different things, so outgoing and so carefree. I definitely wouldn’t have thought I would be writing this now and be so passionate about a STEM start-up initiative – but hey here I am.

I want to simply tell you to make the steps each day in your life – trust me I know it can be hard – to say YES. I am part of an F1 in Schools Team preparing for an International Competition and you know how that started?… well, a teacher came up to me and said Jemma, you want to try this out I think you could be a good fit – inside I thought ahhh I’m not sure, plus I know nothing about F1 and don’t hang out with the types of people doing it – instead for some reason I uttered the word YES. Those people who I wasn’t too sure about are my closest friends, and these friendships have only grown. After going to the Nationals Competition somehow I managed to make my way around introducing myself, actually lost my voice in the process (that’s a whole other story), and became friends with someone representing/from every single state in Australia. Now, you might be thinking good for you, you are just an outgoing person – but I wasn’t like this… I chose one day to have fun and live life, because ultimately as weird and wacky as it is, life will come to an end.

STEM may appear to be full of weird opportunities that perhaps ‘aren’t for you’. But let me just tell you this: from all the crazy experiences I have had with the things I have been involved with, I have never ever regretted it… rather its made the best memories, best friendships and is an everlasting memory. What could go wrong? Say YES to opportunities.

    - Jemma
